REPORT CORRECTIONS — Client vs Rececionista
============================================

The system is INTERNAL to the workshop. Clients never log in and never use the app.

WRONG in report:
- "Client logs in" — clients have no account, no credentials, no session
- "Client registers OS" — clients cannot create service orders

CORRECT:
- System users are: Administrador, Gerente de Loja, Rececionista, Mecânico
- The Rececionista registers the client in the system (if not already there)
- The Rececionista creates the OS when the client walks in with their scooter
- The client is a passive data entity, not a system actor
- The only outbound interaction with the client is the email notification when the scooter is ready (sent automatically by the system)

Fix all use cases and user stories that have "Cliente" as an actor performing system actions.
The client only appears as an external entity that triggers interactions by walking into the shop.
